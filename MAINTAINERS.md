## Becoming a maintainer

We add a maintainer when:

1. They've landed multiple non-trivial MRs over a sustained period.
2. They've reviewed others' MRs in their area of focus.
3. Existing maintainers reach lazy consensus to extend the role.

If that describes you, open an issue and tag the existing
maintainers — we're happy to share the keys.

## Stepping down

A maintainer who no longer has the time to participate is encouraged
to move themselves to an "Emeritus" section at the bottom of this file
in a small MR. No drama, no permission required.
